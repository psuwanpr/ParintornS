I believe in using storytelling for soft power – to inspire and influence people's values in order to create a brighter, more conscious and equitable world. I am driven to explore the human condition and tell stories that challenge the status quo, push boundaries and explore uncharted territories. My work often questions how we can seek and find hope in a world of lovelessness, death, and suffering. My latest project is a coming-of-age film titled, “Rank Me”. Through this film, I am exploring the journey of self-fulfillment and the communal need for love and understanding.
